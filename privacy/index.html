Privacy

The short version: this site doesn’t collect anything about you, and that’s on purpose.

What this site collects

Nothing. There are no accounts, no forms, no cookies, no analytics, and no trackers. The page is a single static file — it runs entirely in your browser and phones nothing home. Sound and animation preferences you toggle live only for the visit and are never stored or sent anywhere.

Hosting

The site is served by Netlify. Like most hosts, Netlify keeps standard, short-lived server logs (things like IP address and browser type) to run and secure their platform. That happens at the hosting layer, not something this site adds; Netlify describes it in their own privacy policy.

Links to other places

The desk links out to other sites I’ve made (and GitHub). Once you leave, their privacy practices apply, not this page’s.

Questions

If something here seems off, tell me — the friendliest route is an issue or message via github.com/kmay89.
